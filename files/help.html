Welcome to the Shopit Help Center. Here you can find answers to frequently asked questions and contact our support team for further assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

To place an order, browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay using various payment methods.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment options.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. Use it to track your order on our website.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Please visit our Returns Page for more details.
